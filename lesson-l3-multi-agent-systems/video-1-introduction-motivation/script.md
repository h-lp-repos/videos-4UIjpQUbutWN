# Video 1: Introduction & Motivation — Why Multi-Agent Systems?

## High-Level Script (with Timestamps & Talking Points)

### [0:00–0:45] Opening Hook
- Pose a motivating question: “What happens when a single AI agent can’t manage the complexity of real-world workflows?”
- Introduce multi-agent systems as the scalable solution.
- **Recording note:** Display a visual demonstrating a single-agent chatbot failing to correctly answer both HR and Tech queries simultaneously.

### [0:45–2:00] Real-World Motivation
- Present an internal support assistant scenario routing HR and technical inquiries to specialized agents.
- Highlight limitations of single-agent systems: lack of specialization, scalability challenges.
- **On-screen:** Animated diagram contrasting single-agent routing versus multi-agent distributed routing.

### [2:00–3:30] What Are Multi-Agent Systems?
- Define multi-agent systems within AI engineering.
- Describe benefits: distributed reasoning, modularity, scalability, maintainability.
- **On-screen:** List of benefits supported by icons.

### [3:30–4:30] Learning Objectives
- Distinguish single-agent and multi-agent architectures.
- Understand collaboration patterns: role assignment, task delegation, conversational handoffs.
- Implement multi-agent workflows using LangChain and OpenAI function calling.
- **On-screen:** Bullet list of objectives.

### [4:30–6:00] Relevance and Industry Context
- Emphasize the importance of multi-agent systems in scalable AI production.
- Preview the hands-on, practical approach of the class.
- **Recording note:** Close with a call to action: “Let’s see how multi-agent systems unlock new AI capabilities.”
